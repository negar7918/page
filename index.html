Geometry of tumor state

Reading tumor evolution off the shape of the data.

I build geometric and Bayesian machine learning methods that integrate multi-omic measurements — transcriptomic, epigenomic, and beyond — into a single coherent picture of how tumor states evolve. My current work treats tumor subtype progression as a trajectory on a curved latent manifold, rather than a cluster label.
